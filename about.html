Lauren is a senior at the University of California, Los Angeles where she is an Art major with a concentration in Painting in Drawing. Although studying studio art, she is interested in pursuing a career in design. One of her biggest interests is the melding of fine art with design and how they are interrelated, in addition to the history behind contemporary graphic design. Her design work is most inspired by Swiss Design, de Stijl, Bauhaus, and Russian Constructivism due to their focus on the two simplest components of both art and design: color and composition.
