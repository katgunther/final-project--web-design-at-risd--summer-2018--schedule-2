editorial design

Sun + Moon, a conceptual travel magazine, was created in an Adobe InDesign course through UC Berkeley. The style is meant to be minimal, with white being a major component throughout the publication. The main ideas behind this magazine were to create something that looks light, is aesthetically pleasing, and provides information in a stylish, yet informed way.
